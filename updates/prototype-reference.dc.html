Deen o Dunya Planner · Prototype

Universal prayer times — local calculation update

Times now compute for the device's actual location with a selectable calculation method (ICNA kept as the North-America default), Asr madhhab, and high-latitude rule. Sunrise & sunset show live on the Today screen. Tap through the phone — the Today screen has two looks to compare, and changing the method on the Location screen updates every time instantly.

The prototype computes times with a real solar algorithm (PrayTimes-equivalent) so method & madhhab changes are live.
