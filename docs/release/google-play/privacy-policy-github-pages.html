Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Deen o Dunya Planner is developed and maintained by Muhammad Jawaid. The app is designed as a personal prayer, Qur'an, azkar, qibla and planning app. The app stores your preferences and downloaded reading data locally on your device.

Information The App Uses

The app may request access to your approximate location to calculate prayer times and qibla direction. It may also request device orientation data for the qibla compass. This information is used for app functionality and is not sold.

Local Storage

The app may save settings, offline Qur'an text, tafsir text and reading preferences in your browser or app storage so the app can work offline.

Third-Party Services

The app may fetch Qur'an text, translations, tafsir, hadith, recitation audio, adhan audio or fonts from third-party services. Those services may receive standard network information such as IP address and request metadata when content is downloaded.

Data Sharing

The app does not sell personal or sensitive user data. If third-party content services are used, data handling by those services is governed by their own policies.

Data Retention

Locally stored app data remains on your device until you clear browser/app storage or uninstall the app.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer, Muhammad Jawaid, at mjawaid.ca@gmail.com.
