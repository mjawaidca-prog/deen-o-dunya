## Full Description

Deen o Dunya Planner helps you organize your day around faith, focus, and personal balance.

Use the app to plan daily routines, follow prayer-time reminders, read Qur'an content, access azkar, check qibla direction, and keep your spiritual and everyday priorities together in one simple planner.

Designed for personal use, Deen o Dunya Planner supports offline-friendly local storage for preferences and reading data where available. Some content, such as Qur'an text, translations, tafsir, hadith, recitation audio, adhan audio, or fonts, may be fetched from third-party services when needed.

Key features:

- Daily planning for faith and personal balance
- Prayer-time support
- Qur'an reading and related content
- Azkar access
- Qibla direction support
- Offline-friendly local preferences and reading data

Deen o Dunya Planner is maintained by Muhammad Jawaid.
