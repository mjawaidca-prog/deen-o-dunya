# App Store Review Notes Draft

Deen o Dunya Planner is a mobile-first Islamic daily planner. It includes prayer times, Qur'an reading with translation and tafsir, offline saved tafsir, qibla direction, azkar, adhan audio controls, and personal Deen/Dunya task planning.

The app does not require login.

Location is used only to calculate prayer times and qibla direction. Device orientation is used only for the qibla compass. User preferences and downloaded tafsir are stored locally on the device for offline use.

Suggested reviewer flow:

1. Open Today to view prayer times and the next prayer.
2. Open Qur'an, choose a surah, turn on Tafsir, and use Download for Offline.
3. Open Qibla and allow location/orientation permissions.
4. Open Azkar and Settings.

No purchases, subscriptions, ads, or account credentials are required.
