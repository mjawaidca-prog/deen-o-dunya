# Google Play Data Safety Notes

Use these notes when filling Google Play Console Data Safety.

## Data Collected

The app itself stores preferences, downloaded tafsir, reading state, and planner data locally on the user's device. It does not sell this data.

## Location

Location may be requested for prayer times and qibla direction. It should be disclosed as app functionality, not advertising or analytics.

## Device Or Other IDs

The app does not intentionally collect advertising IDs.

## Third-Party Network Requests

The app may request Qur'an text, tafsir, audio, and fonts from third-party services. Those services may receive normal network metadata.

## Security Practices

- The app should be served over HTTPS.
- User settings and offline data remain on-device unless the user clears app/browser storage.
- No login is required.

## Play Console Notes

Use `https://YOUR-GITHUB-USERNAME.github.io/deen-o-dunya/privacy.html` as the privacy policy URL after publishing the GitHub Pages site.
