## Full Description

Deen o Dunya Planner helps you organize your day around prayer, Qur'an, reflection, and practical tasks.

Created by Muhammad Jawaid, the app is designed to feel calm, clear, and mobile-first. It brings prayer, Qur'an reading, tafsir, qibla, azkar, and planning into one place so your day stays balanced.

Features include:

- Prayer times and next-prayer dashboard
- Qur'an reading with English and Urdu translation
- Tafsir view with offline download support
- Qibla direction finder
- Morning, evening and daily azkar
- Adhan audio controls
- Deen and Dunya task planning

The app is designed to work on mobile and desktop, with offline support for saved reading content and preferences.
