# App Store Privacy Notes

Use these notes when completing App Store Connect privacy details.

## Data Used

- Location: used to calculate prayer times and qibla direction.
- Device orientation: used for qibla compass heading.
- User content/preferences: prayer settings, downloaded tafsir, reading preferences, and planner state are stored locally on the device.

## Data Not Sold

The app does not sell personal data.

## Tracking

The app does not use advertising tracking identifiers.

## Third-Party Services

The app may load fonts, Qur'an content, tafsir, recitation audio, and adhan audio from third-party network sources. These services may receive normal request metadata such as IP address.
